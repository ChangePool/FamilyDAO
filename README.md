# FamilyDAO

## Executive Summary

If embedding artificial intelligence (AI) tools raises critical questions about how judgment, critical thinking and intellectual independence may change, then creating online spaces where people may interact and connect meaningfully without constant surveillance by centralized authorities becomes more important and valuable to society.

The Web3 industry needs mass adoption. However, cryptocurrency trading practices tend to reflect zero-sum thinking focussing only on the most fundamental requirements for human survival. Currently, blockchain development efforts tend to focus on adoption of blockchain technologies by centralized institutions as a stepping stone to achieving retail mass adoption. Historically, we are used to efforts to dominate markets rewarding business. Therefore, the unique benefits that blockchain technologies may offer to the commons and society are easily lost on the average consumer. A successful blockchain requires decentralization, becoming worthless when a single actor controls more than 50 percent of a network. Successful mass adoption of blockchain technologies requires education and practice related to achieving balance and working towards compromise, cooperation and collaboration instead of relentlessly seeking to control as much as possible.

FamilyDAO offers a socially useful, positive-sum and easy-to-use product for the lay person to understand, learn and experience first hand the exciting new benefits, possibilities and advantages that blockchain technologies offer everyone in the world.

We all have families, or families of origin. We live in a time where more than ever family members may be separated—by geography, emotions, politics and war. FamilyDAO allows you to create a secure blockchain just for you and your family. On your family blockchain, you can create a genogram family tree including relationships spanning any number of generations. Only you and family members you designate in your FamilyDAO can add records and details to your family tree. Together, you can add important documents and records, share news, and learn to vote to make important decisions collectively. You can define rules for voting, as well as rules for creating rules for voting. If family members are far away, then distributing and synchronizing a copy of your family blockchain around the world in real time becomes an advantage by helping to decentralize, secure and preserve your family relationships and history. FamilyDAO may help provide new opportunities for families learning how to resolve or overcome conflict, offering a method for family members to establish healthy boundaries, staying connected while continuing to cooperate on important matters and decision making via blockchain as a trusted intermediary tool. Your family history becomes immutable—shared, preserved and commemorating current and future generations only by and for the people who matter, without data collection or interference by centralized authorities, institutions and organizations seeking to invade your privacy and profit from capturing your personal data.
