# FamilyDAO

## Executive Summary

The Web3 industry needs mass adoption. However, cryptocurrency trading tends to reflect zero-sum thinking. Blockchain development efforts tend to focus on adoption of blockchain technologies by centralized institutions as a stepping stone to achieving retail mass adoption. Historically, we are used to efforts to dominate markets rewarding business. Therefore, the unique benefits that blockchain technologies may offer to the commons are easily lost on the average consumer.

A successful blockchain requires decentralization, becoming worthless when a single actor controls more than 50 percent of a network. Successful mass adoption of blockchain technologies requires education and practice related to achieving balance and working towards compromise, cooperation and collaboration instead of seeking to control as much as possible.

FamilyDAO aims to offer a positive-sum product that is easy for the lay person to understand and use to learn about and benefit from the exciting new possibilities and advantages that blockchain technologies are now poised to offer to everyone in the world.

We all have families, or families of origin. We live in a time where more than ever families may be separated by geography, emotions, politics or even war. FamilyDAO allows you to create a secure blockchain just for you and your family. On your family blockchain, you can store a family tree commemorating your loved ones spanning as many generations as needed. Over the long term, the blockchain grows. Only you and designated family members can add records to your family blockchain. You can add important documents and records, share news, and learn to vote to make important decisions collectively. You can define rules for voting, as well as rules for creating rules for voting. If your family is distant, then distributing and synchronizing a copy of your family blockchain around the world in real time becomes an advantage by helping to decentralize and secure your family blockchain. If your family is dysfunctional, then you can learn to interact in healthier ways using a FamilyDAO. Your family history becomes immutable, shared, preserved for current and future generations only by and for the people who matter, without data collection or interference by centralized institutions and organizations seeking to invade your privacy and profit from capturing your personal data.
